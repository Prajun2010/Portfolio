AI technology.
Author: Prajun Lungeli     Posted On:5/7/2019

The branch of computer science which deals with creation of intelligent machines is known as AI (Artificial Intelligence). It is one of the biggest achievements in computer science. The study and research of AI has been seen almost all around the world where Japan, America, China, UK and Russia have been on the top. It has now become an important part at the technology industry where it is implemented in different sectors.

The core part of AI is Knowledge engineering and machine learning. Without the profound knowledge of core parts, no one can work professionally to build the AI technology. AI technology is the advance technology which has been implemented on all the device nowadays. The best example of AI is robots, which are build todays. The robot is implemented with AI which can mimic the activities of the human beings. As it behaves like human beings, it is now used in different sector like medical sector, educational sector, entertainment sector and many more.

Robots require intelligence to perform different tasks such as object manipulation and navigation. The AI is so advance because of the implementation of advanced logic.
